Monetary Compensation

At the end of the experiment, you will be rewarded monetary compensation for your participation and performance in the game.
You will be rewarded $10 for participating in the experiment.
The most you can earn from this experiment is $24. The least you can earn is $10

Customer Reward

Any setback in capability of the final product will resonate poorly with the customers.

You will be rewarded $4 from customers for deliverance of full capability. The base chip is worth 50% of your promised capability. The custom requirements for your particular product are worth 50% of the promised capability. 100% deliverance of capabilities gives you $4 reward from customer loyalty. 50% deliverance gives you $2.

Stockholder Reward

Any setback in cost to produce final product will resonate poorly with your stockholders.

You will be rewarded $4 from stockholders for spending within $4 million budget limit. For every million spent beyond the $5 million budget, you receive $2 deduction from your stockholder loyalty reward. For every million saved for participating in the joint program instead of creating the chip individually, you win $2.
