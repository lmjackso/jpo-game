Rounds

Every three months, the stakeholders and the joint program manager will meet to conduct a quarterly progress report and peer review session. You can ask for custom features to be added to the base chip capability. You have a number of custom features you want to add. Each feature is worth one su. You may ask for as many custom features to be added to the chip at one time as you want. You will not see what the other players submit but you will be informed if someone's custom request gets approved and how many su it is worth.

Program Termination

The program will terminate when the chip and all its approved custom features are fully developed. The remaining stakeholders in the program will be given full proprietary ownership of the chip to be integrated into their products and mass-produced.

Program Cancellation

The program will cancel if there are less than 2 remaining stakeholders in the program. When the program cancels, the remaining stakeholder will receive full proprietary ownership of the incomplete chip developed thus far, and access to the facility and staff used for the joint program to finish developing the chip and any custom features.

Defect from Joint Program

You may leave the program if you feel that you can deliver your product cheaper and better on your own or believe that the joint program is going to lead to exceeding budget and failing promises for your customers and investors. When you leave the program before its termination, you lose all the money that you invested in the program and any claim on the progress made on the chip development. In which case, you must spend the full cost to develop the chip from scratch.
